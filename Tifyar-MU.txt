Xali ḥsen, winna d-yewwin tamacwaṛt mi d-yuli ɣer Lkar.
Abrid iruḥ d akessar, ihi s laɛqel s laɛqel kan.
Dagi tura aqlaɣ deg tmurt, ah abeḥri n tmurt, ḥzen ay iṭij ur ceṛṛeq.
Ha-t-a lqeḍ ha-t-a uqabub.
Wigi yeɣṛan taɛrabt, r tezmireḍ ara ad temsefhameḍ akked-sen.
D tafellaḥt kan ɣef yiman-is, maca teḥrec aṭas.
Sinistri ur yebɣi ara ad yaɣ tin yeɣṛan.
Yenna-yas: "Aḥlih! ha-t-an yewwi-yaɣ wasif d asawen".
Amṛabeḍ ur neɣṛi, tif-it teqbuct n yiɣi.
Medden ẓran xeddmeɣ tiyuẓaḍ, dɣa ttasen-d ɣer-i snuzuyeɣ-asen timellalin.
Awi-d ma yehwa-ya-m ula d win yegren iman-is ɣer daxel n tesraft, yerna ad tid-kkseɣ.
Sukkes-d iman-ik seg tesraft-a ideg telliḍ tura, sakin ad sukkseḍ wiyyaḍ.
Ih, ini-d d acu-tt leqraya-agi i teɣṛiḍ?
La teqqaṛeḍ teɣṛiḍ, kečč ur tezmireḍ ara i iman-ik!
Ur rbiḥeɣ ddunit-iw ur ddiɣ yidem a yemma.
Ddin uqabac, limmer ad sxedmeɣ allaɣ-iw, ad twaliḍ ma ur d-wwiɣ ara kullec.
Deg yiwet n teswiɛt, yezmer ad yeldi yillu tiwwura.
Ma d tixurdas ẓriɣ tẓewṛeḍ, ulac win k-iɣelben.
Wwet ṭṭbel i win ara k-iceḍḥen, nekk d ayen ssneɣ-k.
Txeddmeḍ ḥala ayen ixeddem wemger i texsayt.
Limmer i tsawaleḍ tidet, ad d-tiniḍ kan teǧǧa-k tmacint d aya.
D nekk i teǧǧa tmacint? ṛuḥ ṛuh ad tekseḍ tizit ɣef yiman-im.
Rǧu tura ad k-necden ɣer tmeɣṛa, ad ternuḍ ɛad aɣenni.
Argaz ula d duṛu-nni iflan ulac-itt deg lǧib-is, yeqqar ad iṛuḥ ɣer ssuq!
